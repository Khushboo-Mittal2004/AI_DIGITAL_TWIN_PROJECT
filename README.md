# AI Digital Twin for Career & Life Simulation   

Your personal AI companion for career, food, and lifestyle decisions.

AI Digital Twin creates a digital version of you—your skills, tastes, preferences, and goals—and provides intelligent, personalized recommendations. Think of it as your mentor, nutritionist, and life strategist, all in one platform.

🚀 Features & Highlights
1. Career Simulation
Build a career profile with skills, experience, and goals.
AI recommends best-fit jobs and companies tailored to you.
Make data-driven decisions for professional growth.
2. Food & Nutrition Simulation
Create a personalized food profile with diet, taste, allergies, and mood.
AI suggests optimal meals for health and taste.
Stay fit, healthy, and energized with AI guidance.
3. Life Simulation
Combines career and food insights to simulate your personalized life path.
See potential outcomes before making real-world decisions.
Provides holistic recommendations for a balanced life.
4. AI-Powered Recommendations
Uses advanced algorithms and ML models for precise suggestions.
Recommendations adapt as your profile evolves over time.
Helps you discover opportunities and habits you might not have considered.
5. User-Centric & Interactive
Easy-to-use interface for creating profiles and receiving insights.
Engaging and personalized experience that feels like a conversation with a mentor.
Perfect for students, professionals, and anyone looking to optimize their life.

Digital Twin Concept: AI mirrors your skills, preferences, and goals.
